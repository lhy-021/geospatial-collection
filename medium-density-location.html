Find Potential Housing Development Sites Back to main page

This was an enhanced BI initiative aiming to find potential areas for housing developments. There were over 65,000 land parcels included in the analysis, covering 46,000 hectares of area, 12 different datasets were utilised to factor in selection criteria like walking catchment, schools, zoing, elevation, flood and other environmental constraints. The result was then uploaded and visualised in Google Maps so that every team member involved in this project could have access to and comment on it.
