This enhanced BI initiative aimed to identify potential areas for housing developments. Over 65,000 land parcels were analyzed, covering 46,000 hectares. Twelve different datasets were utilized to factor in selection criteria such as walking catchment, schools, zoning, elevation, flood risk, and other environmental constraints. The final results were uploaded and visualized in Google Maps, allowing the project team to access and provide feedback.
