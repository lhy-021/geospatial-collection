Find a Suitable Playground Back to Main Page

The goal was to identify suitable sites for building a playground in five suburbs. Criteria included lot area, zoning, slope, flood risk, and vegetation. The result was a KML file, which was imported into Google Earth for 3D visualization. Leveraging the latest GIS technology and workflows can significantly reduce the time needed for business intelligence analysis, streamlining the decision-making process.
