Analyzing Point Cloud and 3D Visualization Back to Main Page

A notable fact is that LiDAR data for the Sydney metro area was provided to councils at no cost. Surveyors were concerned about a quarry in the region but couldn't inspect it due to safety issues. Using Feature Manipulation Engine (FME)'s pre-defined algorithms, I categorized the point cloud into separate layers (ground, vegetation, and buildings) and "painted" it with satellite image colors. The final products included a 3D model (black and white), contour lines, and a relief map.
