Analysing Point Cloud and 3D visualization Back to main page

An interesting fact is that LiDAR data of Sydney metro area was supplied to all the councils with no cost. The surveyors were attentive about a quarry in the region and didn't have the means to do an inspection due to safety concerns. Using Feature Manipulation Engine (FME)'s pre-defined algorithms, I was able to categorize the point cloud to separate layers (ground, vegetation and building), and "paint" it with colors from satellite images. Descendant products include a 3D model (black and white), contour lines and a relief map.
