GIS Audit on Land Zoning Back to Main Page

Conducting a GIS audit on land zoning is complex, especially when ensuring that zoning layers perfectly align with cadastral layers. Misalignment can lead to parcels being assigned incorrect zoning codes, posing significant issues. This task involved identifying miszoned properties and generating reports for further assessment.

CSV reports of current land parcels across different zones were generated using SQL queries, and a second set of reports was produced with GIS software. These were compared to pinpoint discrepancies. For each zone, the audit resulted in a map and spreadsheet output.
The entire workflow was automated.
