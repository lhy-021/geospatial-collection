3D Visualization of Buildings Back to Main Page

3D visualization in GIS can be challenging. While tools like ArcScene and CityEngine offer powerful features, their complexity and the lack of elevation data can make creating 3D images difficult. Fortunately, Google Earth Pro, now free, offers a simpler solution for basic 3D visualizations. By setting site boundaries and adjusting the extrusion parameters, you can quickly generate 3D models. Once again, proprietary software isn't always the only or best option.
