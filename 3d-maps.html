3D Visualization of Buildings Back to main page

3D visualization in GIS has always been a bit of hit-and-miss. Although you can harness the full power of ArcScene and CityEngine if you have the license to the software, the operation complexity and the lack of elevation data can easily deter you from creating a nice 3D image for your next presentation. Luckily, Google Earth Pro was made free last year, and it can come in handy especially when trying to do a simple 3D visualization. All you need is to set a boundary for the site and change the extrusion parameter to the desired height. Once again, proprietary software is not always the best solution.
